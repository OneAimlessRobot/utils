I must not take responsibility for their emotions. I must not. I must not I must not I must not I must not!!!!!! Its their emotions. Their struggle. When I try to change them, I may be good intentioned, I may be mature. I may even be right. But I am also wasting my time. When I step into that ring, I step into a pit full of the traps they set up for themselves and that they know so well. I am fighting the monster in its element. I have known it for minutes. It knows itself for all time. I have to grow on the person. My sole knowledge of my brain will do little to help me. I have to scout their minds first, form an idea. Form a plan.
